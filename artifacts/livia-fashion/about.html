Our story

A small studio, a big love letter to fabric.

We believe clothing should feel like a kindness — soft on skin, easy on conscience, kind on the wallet. Every season we hand-pick fabrics by feel, fit on real bodies (ours, mostly), and price honestly so the people we love can actually afford the pieces we love making.

Our customers tell us the same things, over and over. The cotton is softer than expected. Their daughter wears the dress until it's too small. They ordered one tunic and came back for three. That's the brief. That's the whole brief.

What we stand for

Three small promises.

Quality, always

Real fabrics, sturdy stitching, and a fit that holds up after fifty washes — not just the first.

Honest pricing

Studio direct, fair margins. No inflated tags, no fake discounts. You pay for the garment, not the markup.

Family-first design

Ladies and kids in one studio means our tiniest pieces meet the same standards as our flagship dresses.

Easy returns

7-day returns, no awkward questions. We'd rather you love it than keep it.
